Who We Are


Instant Billings offers comprehensive Revenue Cycle Management services, designed to empower healthcare organizations in achieving superior practice outcomes. Our services extend to independent practices, specialty clinics, single and multispecialty group practices, hospitals, nursing homes, and emergency care centers. This approach drives operational efficiency and fosters financial independence.

Our revenue cycle management solutions are crafted to accelerate practice growth, enhance financial stability, and improve patient experiences, ensuring success in the value-based healthcare landscape. With the latest MIPS/MACRA guidelines in place, we help practices navigate these complexities, enabling them to provide exceptional patient care and earn incentives in value-based care models.

Headquartered in Downers Grove, IL, with a nationwide presence, Instant Billings is driven by a vision to revolutionize the healthcare system. We deliver end-to-end solutions tailored to enhance practice outcomes through operational excellence, allowing healthcare providers to deliver higher-quality care to their communities. This is how we support your success.

Moreover, through strategic collaboration with industry leaders, Instant Billings is well-equipped to address the diverse needs of the healthcare sector. Our team of over 5,000 professionals, including business strategists, Revenue Cycle Consultants, clinical coordinators, compliance officers, and IT specialists, brings extensive expertise to deliver all services under one roof.
